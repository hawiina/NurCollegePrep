Our Services

Comprehensive packages tailored to your college preparation needs

1. Academic Counseling

Our academic counseling is personalized to each student's needs. We start by assessing their academic strengths and weaknesses, helping them choose the right high school courses to build a competitive profile. We'll also work with them on extracurricular planning to ensure they stand out during the application process.

From time management strategies to study skills development, we provide comprehensive support to help students excel academically.

2. Application Review

Master the college application process with ease. The Application Review service provides you with a customized application timeline, support in building a standout student resume, and expert advice on securing impactful recommendation letters. Stay organized and confident every step of the way.

3. Essay Review

Craft compelling essays that captivate admissions officers. With the Essay Review service, you'll have brainstorming sessions to develop unique and authentic ideas, personalized feedback to strengthen your drafts, and expert polishing to ensure your essays are ready to shine.

4. Financial Aid Support

Unlock opportunities to fund your education. The Financial Aid service grants you access to personalized assistance with applications and essays needed for scholarship applications, and expert guidance on financial aid forms like the FAFSA. Take charge of your financial future today.

5. Interview Prep

Prepare for admissions interviews with our Interview Prep service. We'll help you prepare for interviews with your dream schools through preparing you to answer questions about your academics, passions, and future ambitions in mock interview(s).

Our Consultation Process

Our process is designed to be simple, clear, and personalized. Here's what one plan might look like for you:

1

Initial Consultation

We start with an in-depth consultation to understand your goals, academic history, and college preferences. This helps us tailor our advice to your specific needs.

2

Personalized Action Plan

Based on our consultation, we'll create a customized roadmap, outlining the steps to take from course selection to application deadlines.

3

Essay Review & Refinement

We provide detailed feedback on your essays to make them as impactful as possible. We guide you through multiple drafts, ensuring your final version is polished.

4

Final Application Review & Support

Before submitting your applications, we'll do a final review, ensuring everything is in order, and offer any last-minute advice or strategies for success.

Financial Assistance

At Nur College Prep, we are committed to ensuring every student has the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of financial circumstances. While our comprehensive consulting services cater to paying clients, we also dedicate a portion of our time each year to supporting students from low-income families.

If you or someone you know may qualify for this opportunity, please fill out the interest form under the "Get Started" button to schedule a 30-minute free consultation. During this session, we'll discuss your unique circumstances and explore how we can best support your goals.
